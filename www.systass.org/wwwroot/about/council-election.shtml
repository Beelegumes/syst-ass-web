Nominations for Council

The process for selecting new members of Council of the Systematics Association has begun. The term of office will be for three years from November 2015 until November 2018. A call for nominations has been announced, candidates' names should be submitted no later than 20 November 2015. The successful candidate will be announced at the Annual General Meeting (AGM) to be held on Wednesday 27 November 2014 at the Linnean Society of London, Burlington House.

Timetable:
  • NOW CLOSED Call for nominations closes 8 October 2014
  • Nominations approved by Council 15 October 2014
  • Voting opens 22 October 2014
  • Voting closes 20 November 2014
  • Results announced at AGM 27 November 2014 and President elect confirmed
  • Robert Scotland steps down and President elect assumes role after the 2015 AGM
Process:

Candidates' names should be proposed and seconded by two Systematics Association members no later than 8 October 2014. Please include the name and address of the candidate together with the names and addresses of the proposer and seconder as well as a short statement of the candidate's interests. Nominations will be verified by the Secretary and Membership Secretary and the candidate's willingness to stand will be checked. Nominations will be formally approved by Council. If more than one candidate is nominated then a ballot will be conducted and the results will be announced at the 2014 AGM. The successful candidate will take up the role of President elect from the 2014 AGM and spend the following year shadowing the President until they take over from the 2015 AGM. The term of office will run for three years until the 2018 AGM
