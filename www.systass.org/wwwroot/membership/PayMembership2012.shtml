(Step 2 of 2) Pay for your membership

It is now even easier to pay your membership to the Systematics Association. Simply select your membership type, enter your name and e-mail address then click on the Pay Now button which will take you to the Paypal site where you can complete your transaction details. Once your transaction has been completed, a receipt for your purchase will been emailed to you. You may log into your account at www.paypal.com/uk to view details of this transaction.

Your membership is not complete until you pay.
