The Systematics Association welcomes anyone interested in the study or teaching of Systematics. Membership entitles you to reduced registration at our many Conferences, and major reductions on the books published by the Association. You will also receive our Newsletter, and can apply for the Association's grants.

Membership rates are £20, and £10 for students. UK members are encouraged to pay by annual banker's order. Non UK members can pay in US dollars or EUROs ($30 / €30 for normal membership and $15 / €15 for student members) using a payment card such as Visa, Mastercard or PayPal.

Membership benefits

  • Twice yearly Newsletter
  • Discount on Society Publications
  • Reduced registration fee for Society Conferences
  • Systematics Association News mailing list - Sign-up here.
