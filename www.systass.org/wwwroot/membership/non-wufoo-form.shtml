The Systematics Association welcomes anyone interested in the study or teaching of Systematics. Membership entitles you to reduced registration at our many Conferences, and major reductions on the books published by the Association. You will also receive our Newsletter, and can apply for the Association's grants.

Membership rates are £10, and £5 for students. UK members are encouraged to pay by annual banker's order. We can also accept cash payment in US dollars and EUROs ($20 / €20 for normal membership and $10 / €10 for student members).
