Posters

Posters should be vertically oriented maximum size of A0 841mm (33.1 inches) wide × 1,189 mm (46.8 inches) long. Poster abstracts can no longer be submitted.

Accommodation

Accommodation booking through the conference registration system is now closed. Rooms may still be available at the University of Edinburgh's Pollock Halls, but it is now the responsibility of individual delegates to make their accommodation arrangements. To make a booking at Pollock Halls please
phone + 44 (0) 131 651 2007
email
visit the website www.edinburghfirst.com
When making an individual booking at Pollock Halls please state that you are part of the Systematics Association Conference.

Other accommodation (hotels, B&B etc)may be available, but because Edinburgh is very busy during the summer, it is essential that you book very early! August is Festival season in Edinburgh, and both availability and prices can be limiting.

Meals

There is a wide variety of cafés and bars close to RBGE or packed lunches are available for approximately £5.

Dinners at Pollock Halls will be available for approximately £11 each (meal tickets can be purchased on the day at Residence Halls reception), and the city is home to an extensive range of restaurants which provide alternative evening meal options from budget to fine dining.

Conference Dinner and Ceilidh on 30 August 2007, £45.
