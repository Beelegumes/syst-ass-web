Edinburgh - A World Heritage City

From the spectacular hills and World Heritage sites of the Georgian New Town and medieval Old Town to the nightlife on the Shore, Edinburgh is renowned for its unique history, architectural grandeur and cultural vibrancy. The August Edinburgh Festivals and Military Tattoo are just part of the reason the city attracts over 6 million visitors each year, so please book your accommodation early!

Travel

Edinburgh is served by convenient rail and air connections. Edinburgh Waverley rail station is in the City Centre near Princes Street and is served by frequent trains from many major cities in the UK. Edinburgh International Airport is 12km (8 miles) west of the city centre and is easy to reach thanks to an excellent - and regular - Airlink Coach service and good taxi services. It takes about 25 minutes to get from the city centre to the airport for £5 return. Within Edinburgh, there are extensive and affordable bus systems, with routes 8, 17, 23, and 27 between RBGE, the city centre and beyond.

Parking

During the conference, we recommend that delegates take advantage of the excellent local transport system in Edinburgh. We will be providing free scheduled service between Pollock Halls and the Royal Botanic Garden Edinburgh in the mornings and evenings of the conference. Parking in the vicinity of RBGE is extremely limited and is pay-and-display in the area. If you require disabled parking, please let us know well in advance and we will do our best to assist you.

Conference Venues

The Royal Botanic Garden Edinburgh provides a beautiful location for the Sixth Biennial Conference of the Systematics Association. The grounds of the Royal Botanic Garden Edinburgh are a major international tourist attraction and contain a globally important collection of living plants.

The Conference Banquet will be held at Our Dynamic Earth, which offers stunning views of Holyrood Park and is only a short walk from Royal Mile and the Pollock Halls of Residence.

Rooms are available in the University of Edinburgh's Pollock Halls, comprising either en-suite rooms or standard rooms with shared facilities, bed and breakfast.

Weather

Temperatures in Edinburgh can reach as high as around 21/22°C (70°F) during the day in the summer, with average daily temperature around 14°C.
