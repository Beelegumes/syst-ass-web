2015 Call for Nominations to Join Systematic Association Council

Each year we have elections to select new members to the Systematics Association Council. Membership lasts for 3 years. This year we have 6 council members due to retire, thus we are seeking nominations to fill these positions.

We also have 3 officer positions available for nomination. These are Secretary, Newsletter Editor and Webmaster. These positions are open to all members although it is strongly recommended that anyone considering these positions has already served on council.

Further details of the officers and their roles are here

All nominations should be proposed by two SA members and sent to the Secretary (). Nominations should include the nominees name, institution, research interests, a paragraph on why they want to join council and what they feel they could contribute to the SA. The deadline for submission was 13th of November 2015. Nominations will then be discussed and elected at the Association AGM on the 26th November 2014.
