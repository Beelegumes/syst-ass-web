The SRF received a total of 173 applications for the 2014/15 round, with a total value of £181,156.23. Thirty one of the applications were funded at a total cost of £32,136.28. Successful applicants have been contacted by email - if you applied this year you may like to check the list of funded applications to see if your name appears.

A summary report of the 2014/15 application round can be found here.
