The Systematics and Taxonomy (SynTax) research scheme is designed to provide short-term funding for preliminary research that will form the basis of novel responsive mode proposals with a substantial systematics/taxonomy component. The scheme is supported by BBSRC and NERC research councils and the scheme aims to stimulate high quality taxonomy and systematics-related research proposals to the UK's Research Councils. The scheme follows on from the Collaborative Systematics (CoSyst) scheme, through which a number of projects have gone on to receive responsive mode funding. Please note that the SynTax scheme has now finished.
